Real Estate Agency — Mauritania

Manage the inventory and sale of land plots (parcelles) for a real estate agency in Nouakchott — interactive satellite map, sales & commissions, documents, reports and a manager dashboard. All amounts in MRU, with French & Arabic translations.

Everything an agency needs to sell land

From listing a parcelle on a satellite map to recording the sale, agent commission and the attestation de vente.

Interactive map

Full-screen Google Maps (satellite + labels) with colour-coded markers, photo-carousel popups, instant filters and right-click create.

Plots & lotissements

Parcelles with reference, surface, MRU price, GPS, photos, documents and a status workflow — grouped into subdivisions with live counts.

Sales & commissions

Buyer, sale date, agreed price and agent on each plot, with percentage or fixed commissions and a per-agent commission report.

Documents & PDF reports

Attestation de vente, commission report and inventory report, plus typed document attachments (titre foncier, plan, contract).

Manager dashboard

KPI cards, sales/revenue/commission this month, top agents and a breakdown by moughataa, plus graph & pivot analytics.

Localized for Mauritania

MRU currency end to end, the 9 moughataa of Nouakchott pre-loaded, and French & Arabic translations out of the box.

Setup

The interactive map uses the Google Maps JavaScript API. After installing, open Settings → Real Estate and paste your Google Maps API key (create one in the Google Cloud Console with billing enabled and restrict it by HTTP referrer). The rest of the module works without any external service; the default commission rate is configured on the same page.
